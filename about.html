Meet The Team

Passionate developers creating innovative travel solutions for Darjeeling and North Bengal

Our Story

We are a team of four passionate individuals who came together with a shared vision to revolutionize travel experiences in Darjeeling and North Bengal.

What began as a college project soon evolved into a mission to bridge the gap between travelers and authentic local experiences. Our diverse skills in development, design, and local knowledge combine to create this comprehensive platform that helps visitors discover the real Darjeeling while supporting local communities.

Through countless cups of Darjeeling tea and many mountain hikes for "research", we've built this platform to showcase the best our region has to offer while ensuring sustainable and responsible tourism practices.

Our Mission & Values

Showcase Authentic Darjeeling

We're committed to helping travelers experience the real Darjeeling beyond the tourist spots, connecting them with local culture and hidden gems.

Support Local Communities

Our platform empowers local guides, small businesses, and artisans by giving them visibility and fair earning opportunities.

Promote Sustainable Tourism

We advocate for responsible travel practices that preserve Darjeeling's natural beauty and cultural heritage for future generations.
